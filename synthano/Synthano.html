Synthano-ballmills
Synthano

Grinding solutions

This is one of our wonderful products which helps the researchers to design materials exactly as needed, one may or may not get off the shelf and could be crucial to one’s research. To start with, we had the ranges which require operating temperature to be at the room temperature or slightly above it to obtain those desired materials with great ease than the users are currently getting in the international market. Now we will be making available the recent version as well which  will be operating below even the room temperature and will go as low as -40 degree Celsius which is essential for the development of certain class of materials. 
 
Broadly this is popular as HIGH ENERGY PLANETARY BALL MILL. We offer this is in different sizes and with various construction material engaging the sophisticated control over grinding environment. These materials of constructions could be chosen as per user requirement in terms of size and purity/contamination. The environment control could be useful to get the desired phases and preferable oxidation state in the material. Temperature control to sub-zero temperatures is another important feature in one of our models.
 
Available models are SYNTHANO 1S, 1.7S, 2S, 2Sx, 4S, HT and CHZ.
