CONSULTING SERVICES 
It is a service to help academia, researchers & industries in their quest of solutions to their specific problems pertaining to material science.

Our core team, having the background of publishing four patents, understands the importance of R&D for a business to use it as an edge. Moreover, R&D is very time taking, resource-intensive and requires continuous monitoring to make it useful for the organization. We help business organizations by undertaking research & development as per their requirement. Our engagement depends on the customer’s requirement. 

Depending upon the user’s requirement, the delivery of the solution can be as per the following engagement levels:

    • Seeking information 
    • Seeking a solution to a problem
    • Diagnosis for problem redefinition
    • Seeking recommendations 
    • Seeking implementation 
    • Client learning facilitation

Our consulting services encompass the following functional areas 

Basic and advanced materials Testing & Analysis 

Microstructural analysis, mechanical testing, wear behaviour, environmental degradation, physical and thermal properties analysis, magnetic properties, surface roughness etc. Any other requirement can also be sought.

Materials conception 

Type and specification of materials like metal and alloys, polymers, ceramics, composites and advanced materials (bio materials, nanomaterials etc.) will be suggested for specific use. 


Materials development 

New materials from the existing class of materials can be developed based on the specific property requirements. For eg. High temperature materials, bio implants, cryogenic materials, strategic materials, hybrid composites with high strength to weight ratio, energy storage materials, HEAs etc.


Material improvement 

Any existing materials properties can be optimized or enhanced based on the desired requirements. Product refinement will be another aspect of materials development.

Material selection 

In-depth consultancy for the choice of materials to be used under any specific requirements will be dealt with. 

Material failure analysis 

This includes defect and fracture analysis through microstructural observation and NDT.
