SCINOMAT Solutions is a material science company catering to academia, researchers and industries across the globe with high energy grinding solutions, nanomaterials such as nanopowders & nanofluids and Material Science consulting service etc.

SCINOMAT is helping researchers, research institutions and industries harnessing the true potential of material science for their objectives, through its assets & collaborations. Our core team has experience of publishing four Indian patents & various papers in international journals, related to material science. This background gives us the confidence to help you with your objectives in the least possible time frame.
